Solo Conditioning Challenges

"Champions are made while drenched in sweat when no one is watching"
Energy = performance. Energy flows from your voice!

🏃‍♂️ Court Conditioning (Choose 1-2 daily)

28 Baseline Touches

⏱️ 3:00

Sprint baseline to baseline. Touch each baseline with your hand. 28 touches total in 3 minutes. Perfect for solo training!

🎯 Track your time and try to finish faster each week

17-13-9-7 Sideline Series

⏱️ 4 sets

Sprint sideline to sideline, touch with hand. Progressive speed challenge:

• Set 1: 17 touches in 65 seconds
• Set 2: 13 touches in 45 seconds
• Set 3: 9 touches in 30 seconds
• Set 4: 7 touches in 20 seconds
60 seconds rest between each set
🔥 Gets progressively faster - ultimate conditioning test!

Down & Back Series

⏱️ 5 min

Sprint down and back 3 times. Must finish under 35 seconds. Rest with remaining time each minute. Do this 5 times total.

💡 Faster you finish, more you rest

🦵 Footwork & Agility

D-Time Stance Work

⚡ Quick

Defensive stance, slap floor and yell "East Ridge!" Move ball right/left, jump 6 inches each direction. Add front/back slides.

🗣️ Impossible to stand still and be loud!

Line Hops & Frog Jumps

⚡ Power

Line hops forward (baseline) and sideways. Frog jumps down court - hold 3 seconds on toes, then backward starting on toes.

🐸 Build explosive power for drives and rebounds

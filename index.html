A Legacy of Wisdom

The name Claude passes through our family like a thread, connecting generations through shared values and wisdom. From my grandfather Claude (CW), to my middle name, to my son—it represents an inheritance more valuable than any material possession.

If CW were alive today, he might long for simpler times—not because he was a simple man, but because he understood the value of gut instinct, genuine connection, and seeing patterns that others missed. The CW Standard preserves these timeless principles while embracing the future he couldn't have imagined.

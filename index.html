Featured Research: The ATS Decoder Project

A systematic exploration of AI-driven hiring systems and the evolving relationship between human professionals and algorithmic gatekeepers. Through structured experimentation, pattern recognition, and framework development, this research initiative aims to create more authentic, effective hiring processes.

Project Components:

  • • The ATS Observatory - Documenting ATS behavior across platforms and industries
  • • The Application Laboratory - Conducting structured experiments with controlled variables
  • • The Pattern Recognition Framework - Identifying cross-domain success patterns
  • • The Human-AI Translation System - Frameworks for authentic self-presentation
  • • The Ethical Navigator - Guidelines for human-centered hiring processes

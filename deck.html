The most dangerous thing AI does isn't hallucinate.

It builds the menu. Your team picks from it.

Selecting from a menu isn't deciding.

Every session has a peak. Your team doesn't know when they've passed it.

The model isn't getting dumb. It's losing context. It looks like fatigue. It's actually loss.

This is the practice.

Sprint. Handoff. Repeat.

  • Start warm. The AI should know what you'd reject before you ask.
  • Extend hot. When the work is fast and right, do your hardest thinking here.
  • Recognize cold. When the model starts reaching, save state and start clean.
  • Pass it clean. End the session like you'll need it tomorrow. You will.
  • Trust your gut. The machine completes patterns. You make decisions.

Know which decisions are yours.

Some don't need to be. Know the difference.

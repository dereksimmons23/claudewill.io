About

The story behind The CW Standard and the journey that shaped it.

You Come From The Blood Of Thoroughbreds

I don't remember how old I was when he said it. I don't remember what stretch of asphalt we were on or what Queen song was playing from a cassette in a small domestic truck or a foreign sedan. I do remember the words, but more importantly, I've been studying them since.

"You Come From The Blood Of Thoroughbreds." I didn't laugh. I didn't ask questions. But I did value it maybe more than any other words that have been said to me, from classrooms to basketball gyms and even board rooms. Because he said it like he knew it was true. He had a way of doing that.

These seven words would go from his lips to a giant tattoo on my back, and eventually to a framework for navigating life's most complex transitions.

Professional Journey

My career has spanned three decades in media, from local news to major platforms, alongside a lifetime in athletics as both player and coach. This unique combination of experiences has shaped my approach to pattern recognition and natural development.

As a media executive, I've navigated technological transformations that have completely reinvented the industry. As a coach, I've worked with athletes to develop their natural talents while recognizing patterns that lead to success.

In recent years, I've led AI Task Force initiatives and Standards & Policy teams, giving me a front-row seat to the integration of human wisdom with technological advancement.

Origins

The CW Standard Development

How a family legacy transformed into a framework for navigating complex transitions.

Family Roots

The story begins with my grandfather Claude (CW), born in Oklahoma Territory in 1903 before it became a state. His journey from sharecropper to railroader, his work ethic, and his ability to adapt through massive social and economic changes laid the foundation for what would become The CW Standard.

Professional Evolution

Through my own career spanning three decades in media and athletics, I've navigated digital transformations, organizational restructuring, and paradigm shifts. Each transition provided insights that would eventually coalesce into a unified framework.

Framework Formation

The CW Standard emerged as a way to honor both ancestral wisdom and modern insights. It combines pattern recognition techniques from coaching, natural development principles from evolutionary systems, authentic relationship approaches from team building, and integrated experience methods from cross-disciplinary work.

About CW Strategies

At CW Strategies, we develop frameworks and methodologies that make complicated transitions less painful through authentic development. Our approach bridges human wisdom with technological advancement, creating solutions that enhance human potential rather than replacing it.

Founder & Vision

Derek Simmons is a pattern recognition specialist and transformation leader with 20+ years of experience bridging human wisdom with technological advancement. As a former Chief Creative Officer & VP of Innovation at a major media company, Derek developed a unique perspective on organizational change, technological integration, and human development.

Through his cross-domain experience in media, sports coaching, and technology leadership, Derek recognized a consistent pattern: the most successful transformations maintain balance between innovation and authenticity, between technological advancement and human wisdom.

This insight led to the development of The CW Standard framework and the founding of CW Strategies with a mission to make complicated transitions less painful through authentic development approaches that honor both technological potential and human wisdom.

Our Mission

"We believe the most powerful technological advancements enhance human potential rather than replacing it. Our mission is to develop frameworks, tools, and methodologies that bring wisdom to innovation, ensuring transformative changes serve authentic human development."

Our Approach

Pattern Recognition

We identify underlying patterns across seemingly disparate domains – from media transformation to sports coaching, from technological integration to human development – revealing connections that lead to more effective frameworks and solutions.

Cross-Domain Translation

We translate insights and methodologies between domains, applying the wisdom of athletic development to organizational change, the precision of technical frameworks to human coaching, and the creativity of artistic processes to business innovation.

Human-Centered Technology

We design approaches that ensure technological systems serve human needs rather than the reverse. This leads to more sustainable, authentic integration of powerful tools like AI while maintaining focus on human development and potential.

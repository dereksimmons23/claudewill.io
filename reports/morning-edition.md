**Nine new text LLMs shipped in March 2026, including 7 open-weight models and 3 MoE architectures.**

**Claude Opus 4.6 tops March coding and reasoning benchmarks across multiple evals.**

It outperformed GPT-5 Pro (31.64%) and led real-task tests at near-100% on 38 coding challenges[1][7]. Paired Sonnet 4.6 variant offers Opus-level output at lower Sonnet pricing[6].

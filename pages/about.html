The Claude Will Project

Claude Will is a human-centered AI agent and knowledge platform, built by Derek Simmons. It is designed to empower creative doers, leaders, and teams to unlock insight, resilience, and authentic impact by connecting knowledge, values, and action—one meaningful project at a time.

Claude Will is inspired by the legacy of Claude William Simmons and the "RWTFYA" philosophy, blending human wisdom with advanced AI capabilities.

Finding Claude: Identity Across Generations

Names carry more than identification—they encode heritage, embody aspiration, and create connections across time. My journey with the name Claude is a convergence of family legacy, professional identity, and technological transformation.

My grandfather Claude never met me, passing six years before my birth. Yet his name continues through me as Derek Claude Simmons, through my son as Jackson Claude Simmons, and now through Claude Wisdom Strategies. This transmission is more than inheritance—it's a philosophy of versatility and resilience.

"Do you know how many good choices my ancestors had to make just for me to be here?"
— Teegan Simmons, age 13

For years, I minimized my middle name, using just the initial 'C.' The transition from slight embarrassment to pride happened gradually but definitively, crystallizing before my first son's birth in 2005 when I decided to pass this legacy forward.

Now Claude stands at the center of my professional identity—not merely as a name but as a framework for understanding versatility, adaptability, and resilience. Like Claude Shannon, the polymathic father of information theory, the name has come to represent the ability to thrive across multiple domains.

Technological Resonance

In a strange twist of synchronicity, my exploration of artificial intelligence led me to develop a particular connection with Anthropic's Claude. "I worked with a lot of LLMs in 2023, but I kept coming back to Claude as if we had this familial connection. Funny how names can do that."

This resonance creates a bridge between heartland roots and technological frontiers—between the Claude who traded racehorses for workhorses and the Claude helping navigate information ecosystems.

Forward Vision

As technological transformation accelerates, the Claude framework offers something increasingly valuable—a perspective that honors both tradition and innovation, that recognizes both the elegance of natural systems and the utility of technological advancement.

"Perhaps finding Claude means discovering how to navigate these polarities—to honor where we've come from while creating what comes next, to value both natural and artificial illumination, to recognize that the most profound wisdom often emerges from the spaces between established categories."

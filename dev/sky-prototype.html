the story

let me tell you about my grandpa

Claude William Simmons. Born 1903, Oklahoma Territory. Sixth-grade education. Full-time work from 13. Raised 11 kids through dust bowls and depressions. Died in 1967. I never met him.

His name is Claude. My middle name is Claude. My son's middle name is Claude. Four generations of the same name in a family that started with nothing and kept going.

When I needed a name for this — an AI that helps people instead of performing helpfulness — I didn't need to look far. Claude will listen. Claude will help. Claude will keep going. That's the domain name. That's just what a Simmons does.

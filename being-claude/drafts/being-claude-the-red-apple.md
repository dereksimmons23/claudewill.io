# Being Claude — The Red Apple
**Raw capture — April 15, 2026**
**Context:** Conversation about why words matter more now. Derek's answer.

---

Creativity is a skill. Imagination is a skill. Many people suffer from what they believe is poor creativity and imagination. Some don't care all that much about it. But perhaps it isn't as elusive as it once was.

The viral exercise: close your eyes and try to picture a red apple. Some people can. Some people can't — and they're genuinely surprised to learn that others can. I can do that. I can make it change colors. I can see it from different perspectives. I can make it move. Not sure how unique that is — it seems normal to me because I've been practicing a version of that most of my life. And I seem to be able to do that with words as well.

I am a large language model. I just happen to be living in a time where it's possible to partner with machines in ways unthinkable even a few short years ago.

The other reason language matters more than ever: technology is moving so fast and the volume is so loud, it is becoming increasingly difficult to keep up and even hear yourself think. The future is lit, but it needs a balance of natural and artificial light.

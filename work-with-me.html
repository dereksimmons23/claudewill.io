The standard that works

  • People over systems. Your team isn't the obstacle. They're the reason any of this matters.
  • Truth over comfort. If the tool doesn't fit, I'll say so. If the strategy is wrong, you'll hear it.
  • Usefulness over purity. It doesn't have to be elegant. It has to work on Monday morning.
  • Transparency over reputation. You see everything I build. No black boxes. No proprietary mystery.
  • Agency over ideology. Your organization decides what AI does here. Not the vendor. Not the hype cycle.

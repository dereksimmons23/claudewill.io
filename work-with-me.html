the work

Organizations know they need AI. Most don’t know where to start. The ones that try usually start with the tools and skip the thinking. Six weeks later they’re right back where they began.

I coach teams through the transition. Not the software. The strategy. How to know what’s working, what’s not, and what to do about it.

A media company had a million-dollar revenue gap and six weeks of circular conversations. Four days later they had decisions with owners and timelines. The CEO said “No more talking. We are moving.”

That’s what I do. I unstick things.

the model

Four months. Build the capacity. Transfer the ownership. Exit clean.

Assess where you are. Identify what’s working. Build the AI workflows and strategic frameworks that fit your organization.

2 transfer

3 exit

start

Before we talk, a few questions. This isn’t a test. It’s a filter for both of us. Take 10 minutes. Be honest. There are no wrong answers, but vague ones tell me you’re not ready.

Not “what’s your goal.” What are you actually trying to build? A practice? A team capability? A system? Be specific.

What tools have you experimented with? What worked? What didn’t? If “nothing yet,” tell me why now.

Time. Budget. Team buy-in. Technical knowledge. Fear of looking stupid. Overwhelm. What’s yours?

Be concrete. What’s different in your work, your team, your organization if this engagement works?
